ABOUT US

Main activities are processing and freezing fruits, vegetables and forest fruits.

Company Rile Fruit is located in Babicko, municipality of Leskovac. Our cold storage is located in municipality known for production of fruits and vegetables.

Our cold storage capacities are 500 MT and we are able to freeze 25 MT in 24 hours. Every year we are improving products processing and we are constantly investing in modern equipment. We have ISO 22000 standard.

Beside processing and freezing we are also investing in production of fruits and we are investing every year in our strawberry and cherry plantages. Production is supervised by a team of experts who give all the advice to the contractors and so we were able to have 100% controlled production and quality product.
